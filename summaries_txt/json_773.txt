A resolution by the Council of the City and County of Denver, sitting ex officio as the Board of Directors of the Gateway Village General Improvement District, approving a Work Plan, adopting a Budget and making appropriations for the Budget Year 2017 and approving a Mill Levy. Approves the 2017 Work Plan and Budget for the Gateway Village General Improvement District in Council District 11. The Committee approved filing this resolution by consent on 11-10-16.
