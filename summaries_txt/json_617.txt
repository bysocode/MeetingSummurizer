A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 2060 West Colfax Avenue in Lincoln Park. Approves an official map amendment to rezone property from C-MX-5 to C-MX-8 (urban center, mixed-use from 5 to 8 stories), located at 2060 West Colfax Avenue in Council District 3. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 12-17-19.
