A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 1709 & 1717 Washington Street in North Capitol Hill. Approves an official map amendment to rezone property from G-RO-5 to C-MS-5 (general urban, residential-office to urban center, main-street), located at 1709 & 1717 Washington Street in Council District 10. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 12-11-18.
