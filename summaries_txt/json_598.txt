Recommendation to request City Manager to work with the Health Department; Police Department; Fire Department; City Prosecutor's Office; Public Works/Parks, Recreation and Marine; Economic Development, BIDs, Workforce Development, and other Community Resources; and I-Team/Technology Services to prepare a report on the specified areas relating to homelessness and residential quality of life in the City of Long Beach. The report should summarize the data and identify a multi-departmental strategic approach to address the listed topics. This report should include the expected funding required to effectuate a citywide strategy to address homelessness and residential quality of life issues associated with homeless and transient activity. The report should be returned to the City Council no later than 120 days from today's date.
