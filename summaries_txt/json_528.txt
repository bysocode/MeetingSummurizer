A bill for an Ordinance authorizing and approving an amendment to the St. Anthony Urban Redevelopment Plan to add the Sloans Block 7 East Project and to Create the Sloans Block 7 East Sales Tax Increment Area. (FINANCE & SERVICES) Approves an amendment to the St. Anthony Urban Redevelopment Plan for the Sloans Block 7 East Project in Council District 1. The Committee approved filing this bill at its meeting on 12-16-14.
