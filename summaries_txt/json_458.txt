Recommendation to adopt resolution to accept and expend grant funding from the California Department of Housing and Community Development (HCD), in an amount not to exceed $45,000,000; and Authorize City Manager, or designee, to execute all necessary documents, and any necessary subsequent amendments, including amending the terms or award amount, with HCD relating to the grant application and award of grant funds, for acquisition and conversion of a hotel property, and construction of modular non-congregate shelter units for interim housing, for persons experiencing homelessness. (Citywide)
