A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 1901 South Clarkson Street in Platt Park. Approves an official map amendment to rezone property from U-SU-C1 to U-SU-B1 (Allows for an accessory dwelling unit), located at 1901 South Clarkson Street in Council District 7. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 6-23-20.
