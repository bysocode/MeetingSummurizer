A bill for an ordinance relinquishing portions of the easement reserved by Ordinance No. 300, Series of 2006, located near West Howard Place and Federal Boulevard. Relinquishes a portion of the easement established in the Vacating Ordinance No. 300, Series of 2006, located at West Howard Place and Federal Boulevard in Council District 3. The last regularly scheduled Council meeting within the 30-day review period is on 12-12-16. The Committee approved filing this bill by consent on 11-10-16.
