AS AMENDED a bill for an ordinance amending Article IX of Chapter 24 and Article I of Chapter 38 of the Denver Revised Municipal Code to prohibit smoking of tobacco and marijuana products on the 16th Street Pedestrian and Transit Mall and to regulate smoking of tobacco and marijuana products in certain public places. Amends the Denver Revised Municipal Code regarding smoking prohibitions and marijuana products on the 16th Street Pedestrian and Transit Mall. The Committee approved filing this bill at its meeting on 10-11-17. Amended 10-30-17 to give Council opportunity to re-evaluate the smoking ordinance to assure that the enactment is for public benefit and positive health outcomes and that certain populations are not unnecessarily impacted by the ordinance in negative outcomes.
