A resolution approving a proposed Agreement between the City and County of Denver and Paula Castillo to create, fabricate and install three sculptures on the Denver Art Museum and Denver Central Library Campus as part of the City’s public art program. Approves a contract with artist Paula Castillo for $725,292.09 and through 12-31-2026 to create, fabricate and install three sculptures on the Denver Art Museum and Denver Central Library Campus in Council District 10 as part of the City’s public art program (THTRS-202262006-00). The last regularly scheduled Council meeting within the 30-day review period is on 6-27-22. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 5-25-22.
