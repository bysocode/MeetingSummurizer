A bill for an ordinance approving a First Amendment to the Service Plans between the City and County of Denver and DUS Metropolitan District No. 1, DUS Metropolitan District No. 2, DUS Metropolitan District No. 3, DUS Metropolitan District No. 4 and DUS Metropolitan District No. 5. Approves the first amendments to the Service Plans for the Denver Union Station Metropolitan Districts No. 1, 2, 3, 4 & 5 in Council District 9 as discussed at the Finance & Governance Committee on 1-3-17 as part of the refinancing plan for Denver Union Station. The Committee approved filing this bill by consent on 1-10-17.
