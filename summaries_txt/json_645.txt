A bill for an ordinance establishing a new funds in the Grant and Other Money Projects Fund. Establishes the National Western Center Colorado State University (CSU) Facilities Development special revenue fund no. 38426 and the National Western Center Western Stock Show Association (WSSA) Campus Development special revenue fund no. 38427, and approves the associated appropriations of $27.7 million for the CSU Facilities Development fund and $15 million for the WSSA Campus Development fund. The Committee approved filing this bill by consent on 3-20-18.
