AN ORDINANCE relating to Historic Seattle Preservation and Development Authority (“HSPDA”), a public corporation chartered by The City of Seattle; authorizing the Director of the Office of Intergovernmental Relations to enter into agreements with King County and the cities located within King County to permit HSPDA to exercise its chartered authority in such jurisdictions using Preservation Action Fund proceeds; authorizing the Director of the Office of Intergovernmental Relations, after receiving authorization by resolution, to enter into future agreements with King County and other cities located in King County consenting to a request from HSPDA to exercise its chartered authority in such jurisdictions; and extending the City’s powers, authorities, and rights regarding public corporations and authorities beyond the city limits for the projects within HSPDA’s chartered authority.
