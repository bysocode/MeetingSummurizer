A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 8890 East Lowry Boulevard in Lowry Field. Approves a map amendment to rezone property from R-4 w/ waivers to S-MX-5 (residential to suburban mixed-use, 5-stories), located at 8890 East Lowry Boulevard in Council District 5. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 11-16-21.
