A resolution authorizing and approving the expenditure and payment from the appropriation account designated “liability claims,” the sum of Forty Thousand Dollars ($40,000.00) made payable to Condit Csajaghy LLC COLTAF Account, in full payment and satisfaction of any and all claims related to the civil action captioned Edward Lee Hicks v. City and County of Denver, Denver Health And Hospital Authority, Jerome Gonzales, Gabriel Griego, and Frank Rolando which was filed in the United States District Court for the District of Colorado, Case No. 1:17-cv-03001-DDD-KLM. Settles a claim involving the Denver Sheriff Department. This item was approved for filing at the Mayor-Council meeting on 7-12-22.
