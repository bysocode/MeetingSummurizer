AN ORDINANCE relating to City employment; authorizing the execution of a collective bargaining agreement between The City of Seattle and International Association of Fire Fighters, Local 27 AFL-CIO-CLC, for the time period of January 1, 2015, through December 31, 2018; amending Ordinance 124927, which adopted the 2016 Budget, by increasing appropriations to the Seattle Fire Department for providing the 2015 and 2016 payments therefor; and ratifying and confirming certain prior acts; all by a 3/4 vote of the City Council.
