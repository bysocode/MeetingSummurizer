A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 1901 S. Navajo Street and 1900 S. Osage Street in College View - South Platte. Approves an official map amendment to rezone properties from I-MX-3 to I-MX-5, (increases the height limit from 3 to 5 stories) for properties located at 1901 South Navajo Street and 1900 South Osage Street in Council District 7. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 5-7-19.
