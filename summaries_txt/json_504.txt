Recommendation to authorize City Manager, or designee, to execute a one-year agreement with the County of Los Angeles, for reimbursement of expenditures in an amount not to exceed $500,000, to assist in the cost of removing debris deposited on City of Long Beach beaches and marinas emanating from the Los Angeles River, for the period of July 1, 2015 through June 30, 2016.  (Districts 2,3)
