A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 4675, 4685 North Fillmore Street, 4401 North Milwaukee Street in Elyria Swansea. Approves an official map amendment to rezone property from E-SU-D to U-TU-C (urban edge, single-unit to urban, two-unit), located at 4401 Milwaukee Street, 4675 Fillmore Street, and 4685 Fillmore Street in Council District 9. The Committee approved filing this item at its meeting on 11-6-18.
