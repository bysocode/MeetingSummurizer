A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 1400 South Irving Street and 1436 South Irving Street in Mar Lee. Rezones property located at 1400 South Irving Street and 1436 South Irving Street from PUD 180 to S-MU-3 (planned development to suburban, multi-unit) in Council District 3. The Committee approved filing this bill at its meeting on 5-27-17.
