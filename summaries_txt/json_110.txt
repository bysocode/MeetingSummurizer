A bill for an ordinance changing the zoning classification for 1420 38th Street in Five Points. Approves an official map amendment to rezone land at 1420 38th Street from R-MU-30 with waivers to C-MX-8 (residential, mixed-use in the chapter 59 code, to urban center, mixed-use in the current zoning code) in Council District 9. If ordered published, a public hearing will be held on Monday, January 8, 2018. The Committee approved filing this bill at its meeting on 12-12-17.
